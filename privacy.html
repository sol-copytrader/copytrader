Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Overview

We aim to collect the minimum information necessary to operate a non-custodial copytrading tool. We do not control your wallet or private keys.

Information We Collect

  • Wallet metadata: public wallet addresses you connect and basic configuration (leaders followed, copy % settings).
  • Usage data: app events, device/browser info, and approximate region for performance and abuse prevention.
  • Transaction logs: hashes/receipts needed to show PnL and fee receipts. We do not store your seed phrase or private keys.
  • Support messages: information you provide when contacting us.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the Service, including routing and reliability.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or security incidents.
  • Provide support and communicate important updates.
  • Generate aggregate, de-identified analytics to understand feature usage.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or local storage to remember preferences and session state. Analytics, if enabled, are used in aggregate. You can block cookies in your browser; some features may degrade.

Sharing

We may share limited data with infrastructure providers (e.g., RPC, relays, analytics) strictly to operate the Service. We do not sell personal information or share for third-party advertising.

Data Retention

Configuration and trade logs are retained for as long as needed to provide the Service and meet legal/accounting obligations, after which they may be deleted or de-identified.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect stored data. However, no method is 100% secure. Protect your wallet, seed phrase, and devices.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Disconnect your wallet at any time.
  • Request deletion of configuration data by contacting us.
  • Depending on your region, you may have rights of access, correction, or portability.

Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the age required by your region). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed outside your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards.

Changes

We may update this Policy. Changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at privacy@yourdomain.tld.
